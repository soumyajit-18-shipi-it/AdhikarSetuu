# AdhikarSetu

## Introduction

AdhikarSetu is an AI-powered platform that helps MSMEs discover government schemes, estimate missed benefits, understand rejection notices, and support policymakers through exclusion analytics.

The platform is designed for two primary user groups:

* MSME Owners
* Policymakers & Administrators

# MSME User Guide

## 1. Create Business Profile

Navigate to **Profile** and enter:

* Business Sector
* Annual Turnover
* GST Registration Status
* Export Status
* Women Ownership Status
* District

Submit the profile to continue.

### Outcome

The system analyzes eligibility against available government schemes.

---

## 2. Discover Eligible Schemes

Open the **Dashboard**.

The platform displays:

* Eligible Government Schemes
* Estimated Benefits
* Matching Criteria
* Opportunity Summary

### Purpose

Helps businesses identify benefits they may qualify for but have not yet utilized.

---

## 3. Calculate Potential Benefit Loss

Navigate to **Benefit Calculator**.

The calculator estimates:

* Total Eligible Incentives
* Potential Financial Benefits
* Missed Opportunities

### Example

If a business qualifies for multiple schemes but has not applied, the platform estimates the cumulative value of those benefits.

---

## 4. Understand Rejection Notices

Navigate to **Rejection Explainer**.

### Steps

1. Upload a rejection letter or notice.
2. Wait for document processing.
3. Review AI-generated analysis.

### Results

The system provides:

* Rejection Reason
* Plain Language Explanation
* Required Corrections
* Compliance Checklist

### Benefit

Reduces confusion caused by complex administrative language.

# Policymaker Guide

## Exclusion Analytics Dashboard

Navigate to **Policymaker Dashboard**.

The dashboard visualizes:

* District-wise Participation
* Application Rates
* Approval Trends
* High Exclusion Areas

### Usage

Use these insights to:

* Identify underserved regions
* Improve awareness campaigns
* Monitor scheme effectiveness
* Support policy decisions

# Troubleshooting

### No Schemes Displayed

Verify:

* Profile information is complete.
* Business details are accurate.

### Upload Processing Fails

Verify:

* File format is supported.
* Document is readable and not corrupted.

### Dashboard Not Loading

* Refresh the page.
* Verify backend availability.
* Check internet connectivity.
